  Prof Rajat Chakraborty, HOD, Dept of Chem Engg, JU

As the conference chair of ICEIS 2023, I would like to express my sincere gratitude and welcome you to the International Conference on Chemical Engineering Innovation & Sustainability (ICEIS 2023). ICEIS 2023 will be held at Department of Chemical Engineering, Jadavpur University Kolkata, on 26 - 27 February 2023. ICEIS 2023 is going to be a confluence of chemical engineering with sustainability trends. It aims to bring academics, researchers, industry professionals and other relevant stakeholders under one umbrella to share their experience, knowledge and best practices on the latest developments and analyse upcoming trends and problems pertaining to Chemical Engineering and allied mushrooming fields. In this regard, we encourage the participants to exhibit their work at the conference through demonstrations, oral presentations, and posters. We heartily invite academic practitioners around the world to submit research abstracts, papers and posters within the scope of the conference. Additionally, ICEIS 2023 looks forward to be a platform to host dignitaries from diverse backgrounds including academia, government and industry, to communicate the most plaguing of issues and share with audience their solutions based on scientific research and breakthrough in the same.

About CHEMERVESCENCE

The Chemical Engineering Department, Jadavpur University, established in 1921, is the oldest Chemical Engineering department in India. The Department will organise its “Centenary Celebration Programme” during 25-27 February 2023. After a Grand, day-long Inaugural Ceremony on 25th February, 2023, in presence of eminent dignitaries from Government, Academia and Industries, an Industry-Academia Interaction Program will be held on 26th February, 2023. As a part of the “Centenary Celebration Programme” an International Conference titled “Chemical Engineering Innovations and Sustainability (ICEIS)” will be held during 26-27 February in parallel.
